程瑞加(Regina)

Hello this is Regina! I am a first year PhD student in the Human Centered Design & Engineering department at University of Washington.

My research interests are at the intersection of HCI, data science and social computing. My research goal is to leverage human data of various scopes - from data recorded through personal informatics to data generated from online communities - to make positive impact on society. Currently I am interested in the topic of teenager identity development on social media and online communities. I am working with professor Cecilia Aragon on the project of distributed mentoring in online fanfiction communities. I am still exploring different research areas and open to new interesting research topics!

You can contact me at rcheng6 <at> uw <dot> edu!
