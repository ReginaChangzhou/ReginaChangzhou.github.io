I love research! During my undergraduate years, I have been working with Prof. Jaime Pineda, Prof. Steven Dow and Prof. Jim Hollan on research projects about cognitive neuroscience, collaborative design and data science communication through Jupyter Notebook. Check here for details of my projects!

Currently I am applying to Ph.D programs in human-computer interaction(HCI). My interests are in the intersection of HCI, data science and social computation. I am also interested in enhancing understanding of data in the general public through data science education and visualization.
